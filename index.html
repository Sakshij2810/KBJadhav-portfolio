About Us

Theory-Based Practical
Experience in Bridge Execution

With over 30 years of hands-on experience in bridge execution since 1992, Shri Kalyan B. Jadhav has established an independent consultancy practice in 2025. Services are provided purely on the foundation of theory-based practical experience — bridging classroom knowledge with real-world execution challenges.

Having successfully delivered more than 28 bridge projects across Maharashtra for clients including PWD, MSRDC, PMC, PCMC, MMRDC, and DMIC, we offer unparalleled guidance in foundation works, superstructure execution, PSC components, and project consultancy.

Our work spans landmark national projects — from the Mumbai Coastal Road (Package IV, Hajiali Darga) to the Samruddhi Expressway bridge — reflecting the trust placed in us by Maharashtra's leading infrastructure bodies.

Engagement Model

How We Work With You

Our service is offered on a percentage basis of the total project cost put by the tenderer. This ensures our interests are fully aligned with yours — we succeed when your project succeeds.

A detailed payment schedule break-up is provided after a written contract is completed on bond paper, duly signed by both parties.

Formal Agreement: All engagements are governed by a written contract on bond paper, duly executed and signed by Party-1 & Party-2 before any work commences.

Additional Capabilities

We also provide services for geological investigation and structural design support.

Our network includes labour contractors and machinery contractors who are always available in our contact list for day-to-day work at responsible rates and timelines when required.
